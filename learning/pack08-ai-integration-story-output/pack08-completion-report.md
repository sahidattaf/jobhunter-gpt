## Best 30-Second Story

Memorize this. Use it for any opening where time is short.

---

"I'm an AI systems builder based in Curaçao. Over the past five years I've
designed and built AI-powered workflows for business automation — my clearest
example is JobHunterGPT, a human-in-the-loop Python pipeline I built from
scratch that covers keyword extraction, fit scoring, cover letter generation,
and output review. I don't have enterprise platform experience yet, but I
understand the workflow design pattern and I can show you the work directly."

---

**What this does in 30 seconds:**
1. States who you are and where (specific, memorable)
2. Names what you do (AI workflow automation)
3. Names one specific project with real detail
4. Acknowledges the gap before they ask (builds trust)
5. Ends with an offer to prove it (demo)

## Best Proofpoint Interview Answer

The answer to Q8 ("Do you have a balance automation with human review?")
from `proofpoint-interview-story.md` is the strongest single answer in the pack.
It demonstrates design thinking directly relevant to the role. Memorize it.

Key line:
"In JobHunterGPT, the package reviewer flags issues but never fixes them
automatically. The cover letter is generated but must be read and edited before
use. The system produces a package that is READY_FOR_HUMAN_REVIEW — not
READY_TO_SEND. In an enterprise context, that same principle matters more, not less."

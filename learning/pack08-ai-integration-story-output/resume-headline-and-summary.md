enterprise AI workflow roles. Uses the JD's own vocabulary where accurate.

```
AI workflow designer and builder with 5 years of experience translating
business problems into practical, multi-step AI-enabled solutions. Independently
design, build, test, document, and improve AI workflows — with JobHunterGPT as
a live, reviewable example: a Python pipeline covering keyword extraction, ATS
alignment, cover letter generation, fit scoring, and human-in-the-loop package
review, built with Claude Code, GitHub, and VS Code. Background spans AI
automation, business consulting, hospitality operations, and real estate project
development. Completed Coursiv course-based AI tool training in Claude and
Gemini (June 2026, 9 hours). Equivalent practical experience in lieu of
traditional degree. Seeking AI integration, automation consulting, or workflow
design roles where AI is applied to real business outcomes at scale.
```

**Why this is safe:**
- "Translating business problems into AI solutions" — verified in resume
- "Independently design, build, test, document, and improve" — demonstrated in JobHunterGPT
- All module names are real
- "Human-in-the-loop" is a design choice, not a buzzword claim
- "Equivalent practical experience" is honest and relevant to "OR equivalent experience" clauses
- Does NOT claim enterprise administration, cybersecurity, or platform experience

**Do not use this summary for:** General assistant roles, non-AI positions,
or any role where the vocabulary would seem mismatched.

## What Must Not Appear in Any Headline or Summary

- College degree, bachelor's, master's, or any institution name
- Expert or senior (unless specifically an earned title)
- Salesforce, Jira, Snowflake, Redshift, Amazon Q, Totango
- Cybersecurity professional, security engineer, DLP specialist
- Enterprise platform administrator
- Revenue figures or quantified outcomes not verified by Sahid
- Customer success manager or TAM (unless a role held)

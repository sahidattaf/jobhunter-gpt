This file gives you the exact wording to add to `resumes/master_resume.txt`.
It replaces the current `Certifications` section with a more complete
`Education & Certifications` section that covers all three honest pillars:
course certificates, practical equivalence, and project proof.

The current resume already has a `Certifications` section. This upgrades it.

## Usage Notes

**Use on resumes:**
Place this section after Selected Projects and before or after Certifications,
depending on resume flow. Keep it honest — the equivalent experience line
is there to flag you're claiming this under the "OR equivalent experience"
clause, not as a replacement for a stated degree.

**Use on applications with required fields:**
If an application form asks "highest level of education," select the most
accurate option available and add a note in the free-text field:
"Equivalent practical experience — 5 years AI workflow and business automation.
See resume Education & Certifications section."

**Do not:**
- Write "Master's Degree" (the certificates are not degrees).
- Write "Professional Certification" (Coursiv certificates are course-level).
- Invent a school, institution, or GPA.
- Use the phrase "self-taught degree" — it does not exist.

**Do:**
- Describe the certificates accurately as course-based AI tool training.
- Pair them with the project evidence (JobHunterGPT) in the same breath.
- Let the practical experience line do the heavy lifting — it is the
  strongest honest claim you have.

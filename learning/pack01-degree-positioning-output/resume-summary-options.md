## Option A — Conservative

Best for: General applications, roles that ask for straightforward backgrounds.
Tone: Clear and honest without overselling.

```
AI systems builder and entrepreneur based in Curaçao with 5 years of practical
experience across AI workflow design, business automation, hospitality operations,
and real estate development. Founder of GPT Innovation by Attaf. Builder of
JobHunterGPT, a Python-based AI workflow system for job application preparation.
Course certificates in Claude and Gemini (Coursiv, June 2026). Works in Python,
Claude Code, GitHub, VS Code, Notion, and Google Sheets.
```

**Why it's safe:**
Every line maps to a verified fact. "5 years" is confirmed. "Python-based AI
workflow system" describes JobHunterGPT accurately. Certificates are named
as course certificates. Tools listed are in active use.

## Option B — Strong but Honest

Best for: Roles that emphasize AI workflow design and independent building.
Tone: More assertive but still grounded in evidence.

```
Founder-built AI systems builder with 5 years of practical experience designing
and deploying multi-step AI workflows for business automation, hospitality
operations, and project management. Built JobHunterGPT — a human-in-the-loop,
Python-based AI application pipeline covering keyword extraction, fit scoring,
cover letter generation, and automated package review — as proof of independent
full-cycle AI solution delivery. Active in Python, Claude Code, GitHub, and VS Code.
Completed Coursiv AI tool training in Claude and Gemini (June 2026, 9 hours total).
Multilingual: English, Papiamentu, Dutch, Spanish.
```

**Why it's safe:**
"Multi-step AI workflows" is in the current resume. "Human-in-the-loop" is a
specific design choice in JobHunterGPT, not a claim. The module list is real.
"Full-cycle AI solution delivery" describes what JobHunterGPT demonstrates —
it does not claim enterprise scale.

## Option C — Proofpoint-Style AI Integration Positioning

Best for: AI Integration Specialist and similar enterprise AI workflow roles.
Tone: Uses the JD's vocabulary while staying accurate to what Sahid has built.

```
AI workflow designer and builder with 5 years of experience translating
business problems into practical, multi-step AI solutions. Independently
design, build, test, document, and improve AI-enabled workflows — with
JobHunterGPT as a live, reviewable example: a Python pipeline covering
keyword extraction, ATS alignment, cover letter generation, fit scoring,
and output review, built with Claude Code, GitHub, and VS Code. Background
spans AI automation, business development, hospitality operations, and
real estate project development. Completed Coursiv course-based AI tool
training in Claude and Gemini (June 2026). Seeking AI integration,
automation consulting, or workflow design roles with teams applying AI
to real business outcomes.
```

**Why it's safe:**
"Translating business problems into practical AI solutions" is the direct
overlap identified in human-review-notes.txt Section 2. "Independently
design, build, test, document, and improve" matches the JD's description
of the role and matches what Sahid demonstrably does. The module list is
verified. The target role statement is honest and specific.

**Warning:** This option is most aggressive. Only use it if you are
applying for a role that expects this language. Do not use it for general
applications where it will feel out of place.

## Recommendation

Start with **Option B**. It is the strongest honest version without
the context-specific framing of Option C. Upgrade to Option C only when
applying to AI integration or enterprise AI workflow roles specifically.

If you are unsure, default to **Option A** — a clear, simple summary
is never wrong.

---

## What Must Not Appear in Any Summary

- College degree, bachelor's, master's, or any specific institution
- Enterprise platform experience (Amazon Q, Salesforce, Jira, Snowflake, Redshift)
- Cybersecurity background or security domain experience
- Years of experience beyond what can be documented with specific roles
- "Expert" in any tool (claim proficiency or active use instead)
- Revenue figures or quantified outcomes not confirmed by Sahid
